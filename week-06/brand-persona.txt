Brand Persona: Melio Serenity
Personality/Tone: Modern Organic Luxury & Calming. The brand speaks the language of pure aesthetics and tranquility, offering a sophisticated urban oasis for those who value silence and high-end comfort.

Color Palette:

#FBF9F6 (Warm Ivory) — The primary background color, creating a sense of space, cleanliness, and natural golden light.

#738A77 (Soft Sage) — An organic accent color connecting the design to nature and the ocean breeze, perfect for subtle highlights and buttons.

#2C3335 (Deep Anthracite) — A soft, dark shade for headings and body text, offering a more premium and legible alternative to harsh black.

Typography (Google Fonts):

Headings: Montserrat — A clean, geometric sans-serif font that conveys a premium, modern, and open feel.

Body Text: Lato — A highly readable, friendly sans-serif that balances the structure of the headings while maintaining a contemporary look.

Marketing Pitch: Discover Melio Serenity, a refined sanctuary where modern luxury meets the rhythmic calm of the ocean. From sunlit suites featuring soft sage accents to the exquisite culinary journey at Ethos, every detail is curated for those who value true aesthetics and absolute peace.
