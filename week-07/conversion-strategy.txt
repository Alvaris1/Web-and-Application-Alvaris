1. The Value Exchange: I offered a student discount code for hotel services in exchange for the user's email address.
2. Data Minimization: I kept the form simple with only necessary fields: Name, Email, and a University dropdown. The 'Name' allows for personalized communication, the 'Email Address' is essential for delivering the discount code, and the 'University' dropdown (Charles University, CTU, VSE) helps verify their student status and track which local students are most interested.
3. Audit Results: My initial Performance score before optimization was quite low (in the red/orange zone). The "biggest offender" identified by the Lighthouse audit was large, unoptimized images. To fix this, I used the Smush plugin to compress the images, which successfully improved my final score to 67.
