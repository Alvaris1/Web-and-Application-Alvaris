1. The Friction Test: How many clicks does it take from landing on your homepage to completing an order?

It took exactly 6 clicks: 1 to open the "Book Online" page, 1 to select the Deluxe Room, 1 to choose the Bed Type, 1 to add it to the cart, 1 to proceed to checkout, and 1 to place the order.

2. Product Logic: Describe your "Variable" product. What options did you give the user?

My variable product is the "Deluxe Room". I gave the user the choice of Bed Type, with two specific options: "Double" (one large bed) and "Twin" (two separate beds), each with its own pricing.

3. Inventory: What happened to the "Presedentional Suite" page after you placed a test order for one?

 After placing the order, the stock for the "Presidential Suite" automatically decreased from 5 to 4 units. Since the stock is now low, the product page now displays a "Low Stock" warning to create urgency for other guests.
